About Me

个人简介

Hi! I am a PhD student in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at Stony Brook University, working with Prof. Mengkun Liu in the Ultrafast & Near-field Infrared Laboratory (UNI-Lab).

Before joining Stony Brook, I received two B.S. in Physics from a joint program between Stony Brook University and Anhui University in 2024

Research Interests

Research

研究方向

Non-equilibrium dynamics in quantum materials

量子材料中的非平衡动力学

Developing new numerical methods to study the time evolution of strongly correlated systems under external perturbations.

This project involves:

  • Developing tensor network algorithms
  • Implementing parallel computing techniques
  • Analyzing experimental data

开发新的数值方法来研究强关联系统在外界扰动下的时间演化。

本项目包括：

  • 开发张量网络算法
  • 实现并行计算技术
  • 分析实验数据

Machine learning for electronic structure

电子结构的机器学习

Applying neural networks to predict material properties and accelerate first-principles calculations.

应用神经网络预测材料性质并加速第一性原理计算。

Topological superconductivity

拓扑超导性

Investigated the emergence of Majorana fermions in hybrid semiconductor-superconductor nanostructures.

研究了混合半导体-超导体纳米结构中马约拉纳费米子的出现。

Quantum transport

量子输运

Studied the effects of disorder on conductance in Weyl semimetals.

研究了无序对Weyl半金属中电导的影响。
