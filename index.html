About Me

Hi! I am a PhD student in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at Stony Brook University, working with Prof. Mengkun Liu in the Ultrafast & Near-field Infrared Laboratory (UNI-Lab)

I believe that the most profound discoveries often lie at the intersection of disciplines, so more importantly, I'm a grandmaster Starcraft II player (top 50 on the America server). Also, I am aiming to become a pro fingerstyle player!
